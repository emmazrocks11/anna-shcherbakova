  • Hometown: Moscow, Russia

Biography

Trusova is renowned for her exceptional quadruple-jumps, and made history as the first female skater to successfully land a quad lutz, flip, and toe loop in competition. She is also the first to achieve two — later three — quads in a free skate program. She is the Beijing 2022 Olympic silver medallist, a bronze medallist at the 2021 World Figure Skating Championships, and a bronze medallist at the 2020 European Championships.

Records and Notable Acheivements

At 13 years old, born on the 23 June, she is the youngest woman to win at the Junior World Championships and Junior Grand Prix Final

First woman to land a quad Lutz jump in international competition.

First woman to land a quad in combination (the quad toe loop + triple toe loop).

First woman to land a quad toe loop.

First woman to land two quads in the free skate.

First woman to land two different types of quads.

Second woman to land a clean quad Salchow behind Miki Ando.

She became the first woman to attempt 5 quads in her free skate

She became the first woman to land a quad flip in a competition

She set a new record of the highest valued single jump where she scored a 15.71 on her quad flip

She became the first woman ever to land 4 and 5 quads in competition

First and currently, the only woman to land 3 and 4 different types of quads
